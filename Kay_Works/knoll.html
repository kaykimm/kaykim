KNOLL RESEARCH LIBRARY

2025

[Knoll Research Library] is a rebranding project for a research initiative by Knoll, a furniture company. The project includes a redesigned logo, motion assets, and a visual guideline.

The visual concept stems from the idea that every space begins with a corner—a quiet yet defining point that shapes how we inhabit and understand our surroundings. This starting point guided the branding, drawing from Knoll’s long-standing relationship with space, structure, and furniture.
