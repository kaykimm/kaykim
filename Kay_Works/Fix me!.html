FIX ME, FIX ME!

2025

Fix me, Fix me! There are countless books in the world, and just as many types of errors. As a graphic design student, she has designed an irritating book filled with typographic errors—big and small—meant to frustrate designers.

Try spotting and fixing the errors on each page! Since she is still learning myself as a student, wouldn’t it also be fun to discover unintentional errors she might has overlooked?
