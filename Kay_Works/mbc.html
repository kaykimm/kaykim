MBC REBRANDING

2025

[Know Your Numbers] is a campaign promoting Measurement-Based Care(MBC), where simple and consistent check-ins help track progress in theraphy. This project features a calm, peaceful gradient as the primary color palette and includes a campaign mark symbolizing positive growth.

The deliverables included a giveaway bundle, promotional poster, merchandise, and a digital activation of the GAD-7 Test.

This project was designed with Maggie Xian, Willow Baker, and Janice Lee.
